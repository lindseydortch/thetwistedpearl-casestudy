# The Twisted Pearl
### Brand Identity, Marketing Strategy & Full Stack Website

> Big Moments. Small-Town Soul. Twisted Just Right.

---

## Overview

The Twisted Pearl is a modern, stylish microvenue and community event space in the heart of Maypearl, Texas, designed for weddings, showers, birthdays, corporate gatherings, and community events. What made this project uniquely challenging is that The Twisted Pearl shares the same building as BrewJobs, a local coffee shop, separated by a barn door. Two businesses, one address, two completely distinct brand identities.

I built The Twisted Pearl brand completely from scratch, starting with creative direction, logo design, and brand strategy before a single line of code was written. The website is one of my favorite designs I have ever created, balancing the elevated, event ready feeling the brand needed with the warmth and soul of a small town community space.

---

## The Challenge

- Two distinct businesses sharing one building needed two completely distinct brand identities
- The Twisted Pearl needed to feel polished, elevated, and event worthy while still feeling rooted in the warmth of small town Maypearl
- The site needed a functional booking request flow that notified the team without requiring a complex backend or third party booking system
- The brand needed to stand completely on its own separate from BrewJobs next door despite sharing the same physical space

## Brand Identity

Starting from zero, I developed the full Twisted Pearl brand identity including:

- **Brand Strategy** — Defined the brand positioning, voice, and target audience. The Twisted Pearl needed to feel like a venue that makes hosting easy, elevated, and anything but ordinary, without losing the small town soul that makes Maypearl special.
- **Logo Design** — Created the logo and visual identity from scratch, building a look that felt modern and stylish while staying distinct from BrewJobs sharing the same building.
- **Creative Direction** — Established the visual language, color palette, typography, and tone of voice that carries across the website, social media, and all marketing materials.

## Technical Highlights

### Custom Booking Request Flow
Rather than integrating a third party booking platform, I built a custom booking request form that collects event details from potential clients and triggers an automated email to The Twisted Pearl team via Nodemailer. This keeps the process simple and personal, fitting for a boutique venue where every event is handled with a high touch approach.

### Reusable Component Architecture
Like BrewJobs, The Twisted Pearl website was built with a strong focus on reusable components, keeping the codebase clean, consistent, and easy to maintain and expand as the business grows.

### Design
This is one of my favorite designs I have ever created. The challenge was building a site that felt elevated and event ready while still carrying the warmth and soul of a small town community space. Balancing those two things in a single design was one of the most rewarding creative challenges of this project.
